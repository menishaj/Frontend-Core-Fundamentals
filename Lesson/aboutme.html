Past Experiences

IT Career Switches

My career in IT has taken me through three big shifts. I started with backend development since it was what I was most comfortable with, working on different projects and gaining experience. After that, I moved into data engineering, exploring how to work with and manage data. But I’ve always been fascinated by user interfaces, which led me to dive into frontend development. I even got certified in Modern Frontend Development and started working on frontend projects. What began with simple HTML, CSS, and JavaScript has now grown into working with frameworks, and I’m excited to keep pushing forward. It’s been a great journey so far, and I’m looking forward to what’s next.
